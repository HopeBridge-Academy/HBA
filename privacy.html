Introduction

HopeBridge Academy is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you interact with our website and services.

Data We Collect

We collect information such as your name, email, phone number, address, and other details provided during registration, contact, tutor requests, or donation processes. We may also collect usage data, such as IP addresses and browsing behavior, to improve our services.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to process registrations, respond to inquiries, arrange tutoring, verify certifications, process donations, and enhance our services. We may use your contact information to send updates or promotional materials, with an option to opt out. We do not share your information with third parties without your consent, except as required by law.

Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You may also request restrictions on how we process your data or opt out of certain uses, such as marketing communications. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use cookies to enhance user experience and analyze site usage. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Contact Us
